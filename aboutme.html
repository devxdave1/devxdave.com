Background

I am Dave Christopher. I am currently an aspiring developer with a deep interest in philosophy. I am inspired by Albert Camus and his exploration of human persistence in The Myth of Sisyphus.

As an Atheist, I often feel somewhat disconnected from mainstream society, especially in environments where belief systems strongly influence social expectations. Because of this, I tend to prefer solitude over large gatherings. Being alone gives me the freedom to think deeply, question ideas, and reflect on life without distractions. For me, solitude is not something negative it is a space where clarity, curiosity, and personal growth can truly begin.

I also experience Social anxiety, which makes large crowds and constant social interaction feel overwhelming at times. Because of this, I often feel more comfortable spending time alone. Solitude allows me to think clearly, recharge my energy, and focus on the things I truly enjoy such as reading, playing chess, or exploring new ideas.
