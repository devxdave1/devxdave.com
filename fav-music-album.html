Nicole is the second studio album by Indonesian singer-songwriter NIKI. Released on August 12, 2022, the album marks a significant return to her indie-folk roots. It consists primarily of re-recorded songs from her "archive" that she originally wrote and posted on YouTube as a teenager under her real name, Nicole Zefanya.

Background & Theme

The album is often described as a "coming-of-age" record. After experimenting with R&B and conceptual pop in her previous works, NIKI decided to strip back the production to tell the story of her first love and high school heartbreaks. The album is a raw, emotional time capsule that explores themes of nostalgia, regret, and the bittersweet process of growing up.

Personal Connection

I resonate with this album because of its unapologetic honesty. Songs like "Backburner" and "Oceans & Engines" capture the exact feeling of trying to let go of someone while still feeling tethered to the past. It's a masterpiece for anyone who finds comfort in nostalgia and the beauty of acoustic storytelling.
