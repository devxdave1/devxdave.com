My Identity

I identify as asexual, which means that I experience little to no sexual attraction toward others.

In my personal life, I do not feel romantic or sexual attraction toward anyone, and I have no interest in being in a relationship. I am comfortable living that way.

The only person I admire is the celebrity Hanni. I have admired her since 2022. However, this is simply admiration as a fan and inspiration from a celebrity. It does not mean I have romantic or personal feelings toward her.

Understanding my orientation has helped me know myself better. It allows me to live without the pressure of common societal expectations and to focus on my own peace and personal interests.
