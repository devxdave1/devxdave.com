Kim Yoo-jung is a highly acclaimed South Korean actress who holds a significant place in my personal life as my first celebrity crush. My admiration for her began between 2022 and 2023, a period where her career reached new heights through international streaming platforms.

Career and Impact

Known as "Korea's Little Sister" during her years as a child actress, she successfully transitioned into a powerhouse leading lady. Her performance in the Netflix film 20th Century Girl was the turning point that made me a dedicated fan. Her ability to portray the bittersweet essence of first love resonated deeply with me.

Personal Connection

What makes her stand out is her expressive acting. She has a unique way of conveying pain and longing without saying a word. In the movies I’ve watched, specifically her role as Na Bo-ra, she perfectly captured the nostalgia of the 90s, making her not just a "crush," but an artist I truly respect.
