My Chess Journey

I started playing chess around 2023, but I wasn't very consistent with it back then. I only had an Elo of 800+ on Chess.com, and I had never played an OTB (Over-The-Board) game. Eventually, I couldn't access my old account anymore because I forgot the password.

So, in 2025, I started practicing again and read a chess book—I believe it was Bobby Fischer Teaches Chess. After that, I discovered the ICC (Internet Chess Club) and started playing there occasionally. Around that same year, I managed to reach a blitz rating of about 2625 and even defeated some titled players online.

This Chess.com account is new, which is why the Elo is still at this level, but it will reach 2000 soon. That is my goal whenever I have the time.
