PHILOSOPHY

Fundamental Inquiries

What is the meaning of life?

Existentialists like Jean-Paul Sartre argue that "existence precedes essence." We are born without a pre-defined purpose, and it is our absolute responsibility to create our own meaning through our actions and choices.

Does God exist?

A central question in Metaphysics. Blaise Pascal suggested it is logically safer to believe (Pascal's Wager), while Friedrich Nietzsche proclaimed that the concept of a singular divine authority has collapsed in the modern age.

What is Truth?

Philosophers debate whether truth is objective (independent of us) or subjective (dependent on perspective). Rationalists look for truth in logic, while Empiricists believe truth can only be found through sensory experience.
