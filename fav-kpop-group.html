NewJeans (Korean: 뉴진스) is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR. The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They are known for their "girl-next-door" image and easy-listening songs that draw inspiration from the 1990s and 2000s.

Musical Style and Impact

NewJeans made a huge impact on the K-Pop industry with their surprise debut in 2022. Unlike typical groups, they focused on a nostalgic aesthetic and music that sounds fresh yet familiar. Their hits like "Attention," "Hype Boy," "Ditto," and "OMG" dominated charts globally.

Why I Stan NewJeans

NewJeans holds a special place in my life as the first K-pop group I truly became a fan of, starting from 2023 up to 2025. Their music style aligns with my personal taste, especially the vibe of their discography which feels timeless and chill.
