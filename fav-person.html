Hanni (Korean: 하니), born Phạm Ngọc Hân, is a Vietnamese-Australian singer and dancer based in South Korea. She is a member of the globally acclaimed girl group NewJeans under ADOR. Beyond her incredible talent as an idol, she holds the most significant place in my life.
