Claire Elizabeth Cottrill (born August 18, 1998), known professionally as Clairo, is an American singer-songwriter. She began sharing music online at the age of 13 and gained widespread fame in 2017 following the viral success of her lo-fi single, "Pretty Girl."

Early Life

Born in Atlanta, Georgia, and raised in Carlisle, Massachusetts, Clairo grew up in a creative environment. She began recording covers and original songs on her laptop, uploading them to platforms like SoundCloud and Bandcamp under the name Clairo and DJ Baby Benz.

Musical Career

After the massive success of "Pretty Girl," Clairo signed with Fader Label and released her debut EP, Diary 001 (2018). She quickly transitioned from "bedroom pop" to a more refined sound with her debut studio album, Immunity (2019), which featured the hit singles "Bags" and "Sofia."

Her subsequent albums, Sling (2021) and Charm (2024), showcased her growth as a songwriter, incorporating elements of folk and 70s-inspired soft rock. Charm recently received a Grammy nomination for Best Alternative Music Album.

Personal Connection

I admire Clairo for her ability to create intimate, relatable music that feels like a personal conversation. Songs like "Bags" perfectly capture the anxiety and vulnerability of modern relationships. Her evolution as an artist—moving from simple lo-fi beats to complex, soulful arrangements—is truly inspiring.
